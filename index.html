Concept 3, refined — the frame stays, the type & color move

You picked the Frame direction and its mark, and you were right to. This round keeps that mark and its grounded, editorial vibe untouched — then rebuilds the type and color from the parts you liked in Directions 1 & 2. Three takes below, all sharing one settled mark, so the only open decision is typeface and palette.

Kept from Concept 3

Changed · Type

No more serif

Dropped Newsreader/Spectral — too close to Bill’s consulting brand. Type now comes from D1 (Oswald) and D2 (Space Grotesk).

Changed · Color

03

Hearth

D2 TYPE · DARK-LED & INTIMATE

Same Space Grotesk type as Studio, but the color world flips: a deep slate becomes the primary surface, with warm limestone type and an ochre keystone glowing inside the frame. The most intimate and premium read — closest in feeling to a quiet, late-evening conversation. Shown so you can weigh a light system against a dark one with the type held constant.
